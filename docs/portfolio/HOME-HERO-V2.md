## Creative Bible rules applied

1. **No viewport without dominant Object** — Folio owns gravity.  
2. **Silence frames mass** — warm ground `#DCD7CE` shapes the pedestal for type.  
3. **Typography is architecture, secondary here** — statement supports, does not compete.  
4. **Decoration guilty** — fold planes explain light via value shift, not glow.  
5. **Poster Test** — composition readable with motion disabled.  
6. **Paper Object** (Bible §03) — archive / editorial intelligence = studio DNA.

## Why this Hero represents Codexify

The studio’s claim is editorial craft.  
Paper is the native Object of that claim — not stone (architecture client), not steel (luxury client), but the material of thinking and publishing.

The Folio is memorable as a **thing**: fold, slip sheet, blind stamp, registration.  
Visitors should recall the sheet before the sentence.
