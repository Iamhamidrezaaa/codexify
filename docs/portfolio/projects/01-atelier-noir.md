| **Statement** | A digital salon for timepieces that refuse to shout. |

---

## Brand Name

**Atelier Noir**

## Industry

Luxury watches — independent atelier, not a conglomerate house.

## Brand Personality

Reserved. Exact. Nocturnal. Collectible. Slightly severe.

## Target Audience

- Private collectors and first-time haute buyers
- 32–55, internationally mobile
- Value provenance and finishing over logo volume

## Business Goal

Translate atelier credibility online: make rarity feel tangible, guide serious inquiries, and stop looking like a generic luxury ecommerce template.

## Creative Direction

Dark quiet. Metal and glass. Focus on proportion of the watch — not lifestyle fantasy. The site behaves like a private viewing room.

### Color Palette

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Ground | Near-black graphite `#0B0B0C` |
| Surface | Soft charcoal `#161618` |
| Ink | Warm off-white `#EDEAE4` |
| Accent | Single cold metal — brushed steel blue-grey `#8A9199` |
| Signal | Deep oxblood only for rare CTAs `#5C1F24` |

No gold gradients. No gloss flares.

### Typography Direction

- Display: high-contrast serif or precision grotesque — tight tracking, monumental product names
- Body: neutral grotesque, generous measure for specs
- Numerals: tabular, horological — time and references feel engineered

### Photography Direction

- Macro: dials, hands, bezel finishing
- Controlled light, long shadows
- Black backgrounds; occasional wrist only if anonymous and quiet
- Avoid yacht / celebrity tropes

### Layout Direction

- Monumental product stages
- Asymmetric specs columns
- Long vertical rhythm between pieces
- Product pages as chapters, not grids of SKUs

### Interaction Direction

- Slow lens-like focus on details
- Cursor never flashy; magnetic pull only on key titles if used
- Filters feel like archive drawers, not retail chips

### Motion Direction

- Almost still
- Micro parallax on metal surfaces
- Reveals: mask from darkness, not bounce

### UI Personality

Gallery vitrine. Sparse controls. High craft in edges and rules.

### Voice & Tone

Measured. Technical when needed. Never hyperbolic. Speaks to people who already know what “finissage” implies.

### Keywords

atelier, horology, nocturne, steel, provenance, quiet luxury, dial, complication

---

## Visual Direction Summary

**Material world:** brushed metal, sapphire, oil-black.  
**Composition:** one object, much silence.  
**Codexify note:** Same editorial grid discipline as other projects — darker register, colder accent.
