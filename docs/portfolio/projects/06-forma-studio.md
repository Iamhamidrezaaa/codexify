---

## Brand Name

**Forma Studio**

## Industry

Interior design studio — residential and selective hospitality.

## Brand Personality

Tactile. Composed. Domestic modern. Warm intellect. Detail-obsessed.

## Target Audience

- Homeowners beginning a serious renovation
- Boutique hospitality clients
- People who notice joinery and light temperature

## Business Goal

Show process and finished rooms with equal dignity; attract briefs that care about material honesty; avoid the “Pinterest board website.”

## Creative Direction

Material library meets monograph. The site should feel touchable — wood grain, stone, textile — while staying graphically strict.

### Color Palette

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Ground | Warm sand `#EFE8DF` |
| Ink | Espresso `#241F1C` |
| Mute | Clay `#A89080` |
| Accent | Olive / aged bronze `#6B6548` |
| Highlight | Bone `#F7F1EA` for inset panels |

### Typography Direction

- Display: refined serif for project names (domestic literature feel)
- UI / specs: clean grotesque
- Material lists set like inventories

### Photography Direction

- Finished rooms with disciplined styling (few objects)
- Detail crops: handles, stone seams, textile weave
- Process: samples, sketches, site — as a second narrative thread

### Layout Direction

- Alternating full-room plates and detail grids (uneven, intentional)
- Project pages: Story → Materials → Plan notes → Gallery
- Index as a material-led exhibition, not identical cards

### Interaction Direction

- Material swatch hover that swaps a detail plate (meaningful)
- Slow horizontal strip for detail sequences
- Avoid 3D apartment gimmicks unless truly owned by the studio

### Motion Direction

- Crossfades between material states
- Gentle sticky captions over tall room photos
- Stillness preferred over continuous animation

### UI Personality

Atelier sample room. Warm, exact, never cute.

### Voice & Tone

Describes materials and light. Avoids “cozy vibes.” Speaks like a specifier with taste.

### Keywords

material, joinery, proportion, domestic, sample, hospitality, grain, sequence

---

## Visual Direction Summary

**Material world:** wood, stone, textile, bronze.  
**Composition:** room → detail → inventory.  
**Codexify note:** Closes the exhibition with tactility — after capital and clinic, a return to craft you can almost touch.
