---

## Brand Name

**Velora**

## Industry

Premium fashion — ready-to-wear with strong seasonal campaigns.

## Brand Personality

Poised. Fluid. Contemporary. Slightly aloof. Image-led but not chaotic.

## Target Audience

- Style-conscious buyers who follow lookbooks more than discounts
- 25–40 urban
- Expect photography and casting to feel intentional

## Business Goal

Elevate beyond template fashion commerce: make collection storytelling primary, checkout secondary but frictionless; reduce the feeling of endless grid noise.

## Creative Direction

Magazine first, store second. Crop, type, and sequence do the selling. Color is seasonal — system allows campaign swaps without redesigning the bones.

### Color Palette

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Ground | Soft ivory `#F7F4EF` |
| Ink | Soft black `#121212` |
| Mute | Stone `#A39E96` |
| Seasonal accent | Rotating — e.g. lacquer red / ink blue / moss (defined per campaign) |
| System neutrals | Fixed; accent is the only seasonal variable |

### Typography Direction

- Display: fashion editorial — high contrast serif *or* sharp contemporary sans (pick one per season, not both at once)
- UI: restrained grotesque for sizes, cart, filters
- Lookbook titles oversized; commerce labels small and obedient

### Photography Direction

- Campaign: strong art direction, controlled color grade
- Product: consistent ghost mannequin or on-figure — never mixed randomly
- Texture crops (fabric) as transitions between chapters

### Layout Direction

- Asymmetric lookbook sequences
- Commerce grids only after story
- Generous side margins; avoid wall-to-wall product wallpaper

### Interaction Direction

- Horizontal lookbook chapters where it helps narrative
- Hover states on garments: soft opacity, not zoom circus
- Cart and size selection calm, almost invisible until needed

### Motion Direction

- Page transitions like turning coated stock
- Image masks with hard editorial edges
- Avoid parallax overload

### UI Personality

Fashion editor’s desk. Sharp, paced, selective.

### Voice & Tone

Cool. Sparse captions. Speaks in garments and attitudes, not “must-have” language.

### Keywords

lookbook, season, crop, silhouette, campaign, atelier, ready-to-wear, edit

---

## Visual Direction Summary

**Material world:** fabric, skin tone restraint, paper stock.  
**Composition:** crop and sequence.  
**Codexify note:** Most rhythmic of the six — still disciplined, never feed-like.
