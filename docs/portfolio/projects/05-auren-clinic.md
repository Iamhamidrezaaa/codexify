# Project 05 — Auren Clinic
## Luxury Aesthetic Clinic

| | |
|--|--|
| **Slug** | `auren-clinic` |
| **Year** | 2025 |
| **Exhibition order** | 05 |
| **Statement** | Care, not spectacle — a clinic site that lowers the pulse. |

---

## Brand Name

**Auren Clinic**

## Industry

Luxury aesthetic / dermatology clinic.

## Brand Personality

Gentle. Clinical clean. Reassuring. Discreet. Softly luminous.

## Target Audience

- Adults seeking premium aesthetic care
- Sensitive to trust, hygiene cues, and privacy
- Skeptical of aggressive before/after marketing

## Business Goal

Build confidence before the consultation; explain treatments without fear or hype; make booking feel calm; differentiate from clinics that look like Instagram catalogs.

## Creative Direction

Spa-meets-atelier medical. Light, air, skin-true tones. Never clinical-blue cliché, never gold-glam clinic kitsch.

### Color Palette

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Ground | Warm mist `#F6F3F0` |
| Ink | Soft charcoal `#2A2725` |
| Mute | Taupe `#8E877F` |
| Accent | Dusty rose / muted blush `#C4A49A` |
| Sterile line | Cool grey `#D8D4CF` for forms and dividers |

### Typography Direction

- Display: soft humanist sans or gentle serif — welcoming, not decorative script
- Body: very readable; treatment copy in short blocks
- Avoid aggressive all-caps marketing

### Photography Direction

- Soft daylight interiors, materials, hands of care (tasteful)
- Abstracts of water, linen, light on skin-adjacent tones
- No graphic surgery imagery; before/after only if ethically framed and secondary

### Layout Direction

- Wide margins, low density
- Treatment pages as quiet chapters
- Booking UI isolated from marketing noise

### Interaction Direction

- Progressive disclosure for treatment details
- Forms with generous focus states
- Motion that never feels playful

### Motion Direction

- Slow fades
- Soft height expands for accordions
- No parallax on faces

### UI Personality

Private suite. Soft edges used sparingly; mostly calm rectangles and type.

### Voice & Tone

Empathetic, clear, never salesy. Explains “what to expect” more than “transform your life.”

### Keywords

care, calm, consultation, privacy, skin, suite, ritual, reassurance

---

## Visual Direction Summary

**Material world:** linen, ceramic, soft light.  
**Composition:** air around every claim.  
**Codexify note:** Softest accent in the set — still rigorous in grid and type.
