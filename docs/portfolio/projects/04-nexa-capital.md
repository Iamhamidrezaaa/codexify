---

## Brand Name

**Nexa Capital**

## Industry

Investment company — private markets / long-horizon capital (positioned premium, not retail trading).

## Brand Personality

Composed. Analytical. Discreet. Adult. Anti-hype.

## Target Audience

- Founders and family offices evaluating a partner
- CFOs and operators who distrust “fintech aesthetics”
- Internal teams needing a site that matches how they actually speak

## Business Goal

Communicate seriousness and access without dark-mode neon; explain approach and sectors clearly; make contact feel appropriate, not like a lead-gen funnel.

## Creative Direction

Financial quiet luxury. Think annual report redesigned by an architect. Data appears rarely — and only when it earns its place.

### Color Palette

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Ground | Cool white `#F5F6F7` |
| Ink | Navy-black `#0E1520` |
| Secondary | Slate `#5C6675` |
| Line | Pale steel `#D5DAE0` |
| Accent | Single deep green for confirmation / growth context `#1F4D3A` |

No purple AI glow. No candlestick wallpaper.

### Typography Direction

- Display: confident grotesque, medium weight — no tech startup rounded
- Body: highly readable; long strategy texts allowed
- Tables and figures: tabular nums, hairline rules

### Photography Direction

- Abstract architecture, materials, horizons — not handshakes
- Avoid stock “diverse team in glass office”
- Occasional diagrammatic graphics over photos

### Layout Direction

- Strong column discipline (8/4 or 7/5 splits)
- Chapters: Approach → Sectors → People → Contact
- Whitespace as trust signal

### Interaction Direction

- Anchor navigation that feels like a report TOC
- Expandable theses — one open at a time
- No animated charts unless truly clarifying

### Motion Direction

- Minimal
- Line draws for section rules
- Soft fade between chapters

### UI Personality

Private briefing document. Buttons are scarce and consequential.

### Voice & Tone

Plain. Exact. No metaphors about rockets. Prefers “we hold” over “we disrupt.”

### Keywords

capital, horizon, diligence, discreet, structure, mandate, partnership, clarity

---

## Visual Direction Summary

**Material world:** paper, steel, deep green ink.  
**Composition:** columns and rules.  
**Codexify note:** Proves premium without nightlife aesthetics — critical for trust categories.
