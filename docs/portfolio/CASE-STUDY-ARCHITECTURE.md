# Case Study Architecture
## Shared storytelling framework

Every Codexify case study follows the same spine.  
Content changes. Structure does not.

This is not a template for decoration — it is a **thinking sequence**.

---

## Sequence

### 01 · Hero

| Element | Role |
|---------|------|
| Project name | Primary identity |
| Industry | Context in one line |
| Year | Temporal anchor |
| Short statement | One sentence. The thesis. |

**Tone:** Quiet authority. No adjectives stack.

---

### 02 · Challenge

What was broken, unclear, or insufficient?

- Business pressure (not drama)
- Brand or product tension
- Why “refresh the site” was not enough

**Length:** 80–150 words.

---

### 03 · Discovery

How we learned before we drew.

- Research posture (what we looked at — not a method dump)
- Brand positioning in the category
- Audience (specific, human)
- Strategy: the decision that unlocked the work

**Avoid:** Personas as clichés. “User journey” theater without insight.

---

### 04 · Creative Direction

The chosen atmosphere.

- Mood (3–5 precise words)
- Typography direction
- Composition rules
- Color intent
- Visual references (described, not dumped as moodboard spam)

---

### 05 · Design System

Proof of discipline.

- Grid
- Spacing philosophy
- Core components (only what matters)
- Interaction principles for *this* brand

---

### 06 · Selected Screens

Few. Intentional.

- Desktop — primary narrative frames
- Tablet — if the system changes meaningfully
- Mobile — proof of hierarchy under constraint

**Never** show every page. Show decisions.

---

### 07 · Interaction Highlights

Only interactions that serve brand or comprehension.

If removing it doesn’t hurt meaning — cut it.

---

### 08 · Outcome

Honest effects. No invented percentages.

Speak to:

- Business clarity or commercial posture
- Brand perception
- Experience quality (calm, trust, desire, clarity)

Prefer qualitative precision over false quantification.

---

## Writing rules

- Short sentences.
- Editorial confidence.
- Architecture-magazine cadence.
- No: award-winning, world-class, trusted by thousands, AI-powered, innovation, digital transformation, +N% claims.

---

## Visual rules inside case studies

- Project-specific art direction (see each project file)
- Codexify chrome stays minimal (nav, type system of the site)
- Thumbnails and hero media: no device frames

---

## Reuse

This architecture is the content contract for Sprint 2+ implementation.  
Do not invent a new outline per project.
