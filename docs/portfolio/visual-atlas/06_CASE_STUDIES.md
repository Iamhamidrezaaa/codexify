# 06 — Case Studies
## Visual Atlas

Case studies are monographs, not sales decks.  
Analyze narrative craft — not tech stacks.

---

## What “outstanding” means here

- Narrative with chapters, not feature dumps  
- Image rhythm with silence  
- Design system as collectible plates, not component catalogs  
- Outcomes without fake metrics theater  
- Editorial voice consistent with industry dialect

## Case study chapter ideal (Codexify)

1. Object overture (hero plate)  
2. Challenge (text with margin intelligence)  
3. Discovery (structured findings)  
4. Direction (dialect + quote)  
5. System (collectible plates)  
6. Selected frames (not device mockups)  
7. Interactions (intention, not feature list)  
8. Reflection (honest)  
9. Outcome (qualitative, no fake KPIs)  

Image rhythm: pause longer than you fear.
