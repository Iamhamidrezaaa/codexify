# Typography Rules

## Families

| Family | Role | Source |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Peyda** | Primary — Persian / UI default | `src/fonts/peyda` via `next/font/local` |
| **Geist** | Latin / English companion | `src/fonts/geist` via `next/font/local` |

Use Geist only for English passages (`.font-latin`). Body remains Peyda.

## Roles

Classes in `design/typography/roles.css`:  
`type-display` · `type-heading` · `type-title` · `type-body-lg` · `type-body` · `type-caption` · `type-overline` · `type-label` · `type-button` · `type-number` · `type-nav`

## Persian notes

- Minimal letter-spacing on body
- Intentional line breaks on display
- Persian digits for editorial indexes (`۰۱`)
- Generous body line-height (`--leading-body`: 1.85)
