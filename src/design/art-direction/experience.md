# Experience Language

Scroll is storytelling. Motion is choreography — never decoration.

## Section identities

| Section | Character | Entrance | Rhythm |
|---------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Hero | Calm · massive · breathing | Line reveal + scroll dissolve | Full viewport |
| Marquee | Soft pulse | Continuous / pause on hover | Compact band |
| Philosophy | Slow · editorial | `editorialDissolve` + sticky label | Expansive |
| Services | Curiosity · discovery | Sticky index morph (**memorable**) | Compact |
| Footer | Quiet close | `quietArrive` | Tall top padding |

## The one memorable interaction

**Sticky gallery index** on Services: a large watermark numeral crossfades as each service reaches the optical center of the viewport. Museum exhibition, not a marketing list.

## Rules

1. No identical reveal on consecutive sections
2. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — sticky index becomes static; breathing stops
3. Motion should disappear into the experience
4. Do not add a second “signature” interaction without retiring this one
