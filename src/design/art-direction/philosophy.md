# Visual Philosophy

The site is a **handcrafted digital magazine**, not a SaaS product.

## Principles

1. **Restraint** — If it can be removed without losing meaning, remove it.
2. **Hierarchy** — Size, weight, and silence create order; color noise does not.
3. **Intention** — Asymmetry sits on the 12-column grid, never at random.
4. **Craft** — Feels designed by humans who understand Persian typography.

## Anti-patterns

Glassmorphism · purple/neon gradients · glow · floating cards · AI illustrations · flashy cursors · LTR-mirrored layouts · template SaaS heroes.
